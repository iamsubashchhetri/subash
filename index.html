A Lockdown Reunion: Finding Family in Tough TimesOur life in Nepal's busiest capital city, Kathmandu came to a complete stopduring the coronavirus lockdown in 2020. My parents were running thefamily restaurant named Chitawan Cottage while they were at ourhometown Nepalgunj which is very far from the capital city of Nepal whereour original home is. We got an unforgettable chance to come together as afamily because shops, school and every industry were closed. Due to his wemust had to live together under one roof in the peaceful surroundings of asmall town during that time of the year.My mom is the main character in this story. She is a strong woman whodevotes herself to running both our home and the restaurant owned by ourfamily as our restaurant used to run for very less amount of time. She is thesoul of our family thanks to her sweet smile such a caring eyes and softvoice. She taught us a lot in that time period and she also supported myfather emotionally as my father had a very hard time during that time period.I saw an change in my father's mental state throughout the lockdown. Hesuffered from uncertainty which left him weak and weak mentally. Mymother on the other hand, acted as a foundation for him and the rest of thefamily. My father found comfort and strength in her kind words of support


She encouraged us to be together and stay positive for the entire time periodand she was the one who made those time very memorable. I didn’t had agood relationship with my sister though I was living with her in the sameapartment in capital but during those months we became closer valuing eachothers individuality and building our relationships as siblings.We had to say goodbye and as every thing was slowing opening ,the wholeworld was opening after a long time, every business was starting again.


Wewere grateful of our time together and the knowledge we have had duringand her understanding presence which helped him get through thechallenging times. In addition to supporting my father my mother also keptour family together giving constant strength of love and familialconnections in difficult situations.We welcomed the long awaited unity at the reunion with excitement and joy.We became used to sharing meals stories and laughs while residing in onehouse. The presence of my mom and dad provided support and direction theentire time period during the lockdown.that time.


















My memories of that reunion still has influence my life today. My mother'slove and insight is a guide reminding me of the importance of family andunity no matter what the future is and where we will be. FEAR


Afraid follows me day and night,

In the embrace of silence, it takes it's flight.

Even in joy, it finds a way,

With every change, it leads my way.


As I grow up older, fear settles in me,

When my world shakes fuel is the key.

When my mother hides me her tearful eye,

Behind the words "I'm tired", I sigh.


When health leaves me at my crucial times,

Fear starts to grip me and plays its chimes.

Yet fear it's not entirely dark,

It gives me company, makes me strong and sharp.


It awakens me when I stumble and sway,

A supportive friend guiding my way.

In the presence of fear, I find my might,

To rise when I am about to fall in its light.
